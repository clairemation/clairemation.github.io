Hi! I'm Claire.

I live in NYC and am an utterly prototypical born-and-bred New Yawker from the Seinfeld era. In the fall of 2015, I entered Dev Bootcamp.

My interests?

Coding, of course. Since I was a kid I'd program little (bad) games on the home TRS-80 or my TI-84 calculator. In high school (approximately 1,000,000,000 years ago) I was lucky enough to learn C in the awesome Mr. Zamansky's class.

Animation and independent comics! I started out training to be a traditional animator (the kind that makes all those drawings). Now I've decided to make coding my career, but I'll always keep a hand in something like comics. Check out my pet projects at http://eclair-voyant.blogspot.com.

Linguistics. I self-studied Japanese for many years and supported myself after art school doing book translations for The Pokemon Company, International (then Pokemon USA).

Swing dance! (A bit deceptive, I can do like 2% of that. There is no one alive today who can dance like that.) But I refuse to learn Bal, so that leads can't wuss out of leading me in a swingout to 320 BPM.
